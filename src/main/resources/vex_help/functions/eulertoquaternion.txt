"""Creates a vector4 representing a quaternion from euler angles."""

[Unit quaternions are used to represent rotations|Wp:Quaternions_and_spatial_rotation]. This VEX function creates a unit quaternion that represents a rotation.

:usage: `vector4 eulertoquaternion(vector rotations, int order)`

Creates a vector4 representing a unit quaternion from a vector representing Euler rotations in X, Y, and Z.

The angles are in radians.  Use the `radians()` function to convert degrees into radians.

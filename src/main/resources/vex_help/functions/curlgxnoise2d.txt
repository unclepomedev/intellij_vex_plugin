"""Computes divergence free noise in the plane based on simplex noise."""

:usage: `vector curlgxnoise2d(vector2 xy)`
:usage: `vector curlgxnoise2d(float x, float y)`
:usage: `vector curlgxnoise2d(vector xyz)`
:usage: `vector curlgxnoise2d(vector4 xyzt)`

Evaluates a divergence free vector field by computing curl of one-dimensional
simplex noise (see [gxnoise|Vex:gxnoise]). The first two components of the
output vector contain the divergence free noise, and its final component is 0.
